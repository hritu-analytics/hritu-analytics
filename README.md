# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I’m Hrituparna 👋<br><br>I’m studying MSc Business Analytics at the University of Glasgow, with a background in customer operations and marketing.<br><br>I work on data and business analysis projects that focus on revenue, churn, conversion, customer behaviour, and profitability. I’m most interested in work where analysis is used to understand what is going wrong, why it is happening, and what should change as a result.<br><br>My projects are built around business questions rather than tools. The goal is not just to produce analysis, but to make the output clear enough to support a decision.<br><br>So far, my work has included:<br>- E-commerce Funnel Analysis: identifying where revenue was being lost and what was driving the drop<br>- Customer Churn Analysis: examining retention risk and the factors behind customer loss<br>- Consulting Profitability & Utilisation Analysis: looking at margin leakage, utilisation gaps, and performance improvement opportunities<br><br>I work mainly with Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau, and I care a lot about structuring analysis clearly and making it useful.<br><br><br>This profile is a collection of that work.
